प्रश्न
m. (-श्नः) 
	 1. A question, a demand, an inquiry, an interrogation.
 2. Judicial inquiry. 
	 3. The subject of a controversy, a controverted point. 
	 4. Inquiry into the future. 
	 5. Problem for calculation. 
	 6. Section of a book. 
f. (-श्नी) An aquatic plant, (Pistiastratiotes.) “पाना” इति भाषा ।
 E. प्रच्छ् to ask, aff. नङ्.
